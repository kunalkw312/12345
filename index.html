Service FAQs

Common questions regarding our data services and lead processing.

Are your leads exclusive?+
Yes, we provide both exclusive and shared lead options depending on your campaign budget. Exclusive leads are never sold to another buyer.
Can I target specific geographical locations?+
Absolutely. Our system allows granular targeting by state, city, ZIP code, area code, and various demographic filters like age or income.
Do you offer replacement policies for bad leads?+
Yes, we maintain a strict quality control policy. Invalid numbers, disconnected lines, or out-of-criteria leads can be returned for credit within a specified timeframe.
